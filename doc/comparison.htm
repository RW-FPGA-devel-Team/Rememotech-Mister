Of course its going to be smaller, faster, cheaper, better. But how much?

I'm not selling REMEMOTECH, but perhaps I should.     :-)

You can make your own. Just buy a DE1 and download the REMEMOTECH collateral.

Notice how REMEMOrizer represents interesting middle ground between old real Memotech hardware and new fully re-implemented hardware, in terms of feature set and cost.

Of course, the price comparison is unfair. Back in 1984, the price will have needed to recover the costs of developing the hardware and the software. I am largely reusing the existing software.
